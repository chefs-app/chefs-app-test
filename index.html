What is Chefs' ?

Do you write down your recipes in notebooks, cut them out of magazines, collect them on a cookbook shelf, or maybe keep them on your computer and phone? How much time do you spend going through all of this for a dinner idea?
We present an application that will make you say goodbye to these problems and make cooking even more fun for you.


📚All your recipes in one place
Create your personal cookbook filled with all your recipes. Never be afraid of losing them again - all recipes will be saved in the cloud on your personal account, so even when you change your phone, all your recipes will remain in your pocket.


✨Intuitive and clear design
Chefs' has been created with your convenience in mind. The minimalist design allows you to focus on what is important. When you need to cook something, easily browse and search your list of dishes. When you cook, check off the ingredients you added so you never add sugar twice again. Add recipes to your favorites to easily find what you like to prepare or you eat the most.


📷Add recipes from photos
Take a picture of a cookbook, newspaper or use a screenshot, and the recipe will be in your personal cookbook in just a few seconds. Artificial intelligence will detect the text, and a specially created neural network will find the title, ingredients and steps of the recipe. Even when it makes a mistake, you can make corrections. The created tool distinguishes Chefs' from other apps because adding recipes has never been that easy!


🖊️Add recipes manually
Now you can add a new recipe in just a few seconds. Enter the ingredients by hand or paste the entire list from the website and we will divide it accordingly. Add recipe steps, preparation time and number of servings. If you want to show off your recipe, add an interesting description and photo of your creation.


💡Look for culinary inspiration
Browse community recipes for ideas and culinary inspiration. When you find something for yourself, start cooking right away or save the recipe for later. If you speak more than one language, you can also view recipes from abroad.


🧑‍🤝‍🧑Share recipes
There are many cooking enthusiasts, so why not be able to share your dishes. You can send each recipe to your friends with a few clicks and a link generated in the application. Additionally, you can save the recipes made available to you instantly.


📴Cook offline
We know that the internet is not always available, so Chefs' works offline as well. Not only can you view all your own recipes, but you can also add new ones without accessing the web!
